FAQs

What is Mall Express company?

For seller, the Mall Express provide you with opportunities to get approach the customers around the world. We are available in 180 countries, if your country has our company branches, do not miss this chance to sell your products around the world.

For buyers, the Mall Express is an international market where you can buy anything you want without caring about the distance as long as your country has a branch of our company.

Which payment is available at Mall Express?

Mall Express allows you to shop in over 80 currencies. Currency of Preference is a display-only feature and allows you to see prices displayed in your preferred currency. You can update your Currency of Preference by changing from USD (default currency) to your preferred currency in the currency settings page. The available currencies for shopping are listed below. Once you are ready to purchase your item, at checkout, an 'Order Total' will display the total price of the order in the preferred currency. For payment, a 'Payment Total' will display the amount your payment method will be charged in.

If your order is eligible at checkout for Mall Express Currency Converter, you can elect to pay with an eligible card, and the Payment Total will display the price in your card currency you elected to pay in. Please note that payment in a different currency is only possible for orders eligible for Amazon Currency Converter.

The currency you select is for reference use only. Prices may be displayed in your selected currency for your convenience, and you can have prices displayed in your chosen currency, even for orders not eligible for Mall Express Currency Converter. At checkout, if you wish to pay using your chosen currency, the actual sales price will have additional fees which will be calculated at checkout.

What is regular membership and premium membership?

Regular membership are the basic accounts you can register for free. As a Standard user, you can do just about anything in our platform, such as browsing or purchasing product.

For premium membership, we charge 10$ a month, they will be offered a 5% discount for each transaction and free delivery within 5 km.

Furthermore, feel free to have a look on entire mall as a guest.

Does Mall Express have a referral system?

Yes, we do, every referral account do a transaction will give you points to earn reward.
